About Elevate

Elevate is a platform for patients diagnosed with depression and are unable to consult their doctors during these times. Moreover Elevate features a better system that monitors the mentally depressed. We assure early warnings of severe depression and daily checkups and intimate the doctors whenever it is necessary.

Features

Doctor Interface

Doctors can signup and add their patients here and monitor their patients daily updates here. Moreover the doctor will also be intimidated via sms or mail if the situation of the patient has been found to be serious. 

Patient Interface

More like a daily Vlog the patient has to self realise his path and thoughts to a series of questions raised by the system. The system shows his report and updates it to the respective doctor.

AI System

The AI system watches for the change in expressions of the user while he answers the questions raised to him. The records for a session are then saved to the database and analysis is done on it. A predicted rate of depression is shown in the report submitted to the doctor
